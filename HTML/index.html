Year Long Analysis

Statistics from the entire year go into our predictions
from tournament experience of players to points per game.

Compare Conferences

Our algorithms rank conferences based on strength of schedule, wins against non conference opponents, winning margin of conference members, and many other factors.

Experience

We use data from previous years to track what makes teams successful each year. We also factor in the how much tournament experience each player has and how they performed.

Momentum

We have devised algorithms to determine which teams are hot and which are not. Think its your teams year because of a couple of big wins late in the year? Check with our data before you go all in.
